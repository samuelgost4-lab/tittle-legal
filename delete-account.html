Delete Your Tittle Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your Tittle account and all associated data using either of the methods below.

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Delete your account directly from the Tittle app:

  1. Open the Tittle app
  2. Go to Settings (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

Note: This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

Method 2: Email Request

Send us an email to request account deletion:

Email: help.tittleapp@gmail.com

Please include in your email:

  • The email address or phone number associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want your account and all data deleted

Response time: We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, username, email, phone number)
  • All photos you have posted
  • Your likes, comments, and interactions
  • Your follower and following lists
  • Your location history
  • All associated account data

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will not be able to recover your account, photos, or any other data after deletion is complete.

Data Retention

Most data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. However:

  • Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days
  • Data required for legal compliance may be retained as required by law
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement
