Tittle requests the following permissions to provide core functionality. You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users (Based on Your Privacy Settings)

Public Posts:

  • Photos marked "Public" are visible to all users within geographic range
  • Your username and display name are visible on public posts

Private Posts:

  • Photos marked "Private" are only visible to users you follow
  • Your followers can see your private content

  • Displays advertisements within the app
  • May collect device identifiers for ad targeting
